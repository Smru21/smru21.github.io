My Journey

Curious Child

I grew up inside the Sri Aurobindo Ashram community in Pondicherry. Most toys I received were eventually opened, modified or rebuilt. Curiosity became my way of understanding the world.

Ashram Life

Ashram life exposed me to sports, arts, practical work and community responsibility. Learning happened through experience.

Leadership & Growth

Through physical education, presentations and community activities, I gradually transformed from a shy child into a confident leader.

Physics

Physics became my favorite subject because it attempted to answer deeper questions about reality and how nature works.

Solar Monitoring

I helped monitor one of India's early solar-powered school campuses and learned how renewable energy systems operate at scale.

Wind Turbine Project

Using weather data and field measurements, we designed and built a small experimental wind turbine.

Environmental Surveys

Community ecology work connected scientific observation with real ecosystems and people.

Aurogurukul Team Lead

During my MSc Physics journey, I worked as Team Lead at Aurogurukul, helping develop hands-on science kits, supporting laboratory activities and creating learning experiences that made science accessible.

Woodworking, carving, machining and fabrication taught me patience, precision and respect for materials. Building with my hands remains one of my favorite ways to learn.
