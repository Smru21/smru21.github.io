Renewable Energy

Energy Systems & Infrastructure

227 kWp Solar Fleet Operations

Managed and maintained a distributed 227 kWp rooftop solar fleet across seven inverter sites. Performed inverter diagnostics, communication troubleshooting, production monitoring and preventive maintenance using SolarEdge and Wattmon platforms. Restored failed PLC communication hardware and maintained near-continuous generation visibility across the fleet.

Solar Monitoring & Analytics

Monitored production performance, inverter health and communication systems across multiple installations. Used monitoring data to identify failures, downtime and performance deviations before they became critical issues.

60W Hugh Piggott Wind Turbine

Designed, fabricated and installed from scratch. Included blade carving using NACA 4412 profiles, stator construction, generator winding, epoxy casting and site resource assessment.
